Power Supply System

Redundant Power Supply refers to a system designed to ensure continuous power availability even in the event of a failure in the primary power source.

Backup Generators

Automatically kick in during a power outage. Provide long-term power until the primary source is restored.

(UPS) Power Supplies

Offer immediate, short-term power using batteries to bridge gaps between outages and generator.

Multiple Power Sources

Power drawn from multiple independent grids or circuits. Reduces the risk of a single-point failure.

Failover Mechanism

Automatically switches to a backup source if the primary supply fails. Ensures seamless operation
